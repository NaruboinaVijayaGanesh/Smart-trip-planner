Problem Statement

Build a role-based AI trip planner with secure auth, itinerary generation, budget prediction, hotel suggestions, and booking workflow.

The app must support traveler and travel-agent personas with transparent recalculation and status tracking.

MakeMyTrip Study Snapshot

  • Strong search-first flow (origin, destination, date, travelers).
  • Clear pricing blocks and fare/availability disclaimers.
  • One-click share links for itineraries and bookings.
  • Filterable hotel results with distance and rating focus.

This app mirrors those patterns with AI-generated itinerary and agent collaboration controls.
